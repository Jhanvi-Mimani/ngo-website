Our Story
Second Kick Sports Foundation was born from a deep passion for football and a desire to give back to the community.
Our founder, inspired by a chance encounter with a young boy asking for football lessons, started Second Kick Ghaziabad Football Club.
What began as a small dream now has two thriving centers in Ghaziabad, providing young athletes with the opportunity to follow their passion.
With a mission to serve those in need, the foundation was created to offer underprivileged children access to football training, life skills, and, in the future, health and educational support. We aim to empower these children, helping them reach their full potential and build a brighter future.
Join us in giving every child the opportunity to chase their dreams, on and off the field.

Vision
At Second Kick Sports Foundation, we envision creating a dynamic ecosystem that nurtures the overall development of
aspiring football players,providing them with the resources and opportunities they need to excel. Our goal is to build a strong, supportive community that promotes
professional football and brings long-awaited recognition and structure to the sport. Through our efforts, we strive to position football as a profession
with global potential, paving the way for the next generation of football stars.
Mission
Our mission is to identify and nurture football talent in and around the NCR region, offering comprehensive grassroots
support to help young players reach their full potential. We are committed to facilitating their growth through a well-rounded approach,
providing access to quality education, health benefits and skill training. By forming strategic partnerships with national and international football clubs,
we aim to create pathways for players to achieve success on the global stage. We are also dedicated to building a thriving community that not only promotes professional football but also elevates the recognition and importance of the sport, making it a respected career choice for future generations.
Our Goals
1. Provide proper nutrition to help children grow, stay healthy, and perform at their best in football.

2. Support education through scholarships and resources to overcome financial barriers and empower youth.

3. Improve health and wellness with regular check-ups and health awareness, ensuring a strong foundation for both sports and life.

4. Guide youth away from substance abuse and unhealthy lifestyles, while creating pathways to professional football by qualifying for the I-League and ISL.
