Kicking Dreams into Reality

Welcome to Second Kick Sports Foundation

At Second Kick Sports Foundation, we are dedicated to nurturing young talent by providing access to quality sports training, education, and healthcare. Our mission is to empower underprivileged children through football, fostering discipline, teamwork, and resilience while ensuring academic growth and well-being. With structured training, mentorship, and community support, we create an environment where every child can dream big and achieve success both on and off the field.

A Day at Second Kick

8:00 AM - 9:00 AM Educational classes and mentoring sessions to ensure holistic development.

6:30 PM - 7:30 PM: Evening football matches and tactical training.
